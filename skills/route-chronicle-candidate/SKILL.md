# Route Chronicle Candidate

## Trigger Phrases
- "chronicle candidate"
- "public milestone"
- "story-worthy"
- "historical event"
- "chronicles staging"
- "publication milestone"

## Required Memory Files
- `memory/02-company-safety-rules.md`
- `memory/05-cross-domain-relationships.md`
- `memory/06-source-of-truth-rules.md`
- `_system/agent_repo_registry.md` when domain or database ownership matters
- `_system/database_awareness.md` when structured record access matters

## Workflow
1. Identify the possible chronicle candidate and why it may be public-facing.
2. Separate observation, interpretation, story framing, and approved public claim.
3. Identify owning domains: Research for ecological evidence, Content for public narrative/publishing workflow, Web for rendering, App for runtime/admin surfaces where relevant.
4. Check whether the item needs live Supabase/App Planner or chronicles-staging awareness before any recommendation.
5. Propose the candidate and owner handoff before any write.
6. Ask for explicit approval before writing `chronicles_staging` or any structured record.
7. If approved writes happen, read back the records.
8. Record only Company-level implications in Company memory; keep detailed chronology/story work with Content/Research or structured records.

## Do-Not-Do Boundaries
- Do not write chronicle staging without approval.
- Do not treat internal strategy notes as public chronicles.
- Do not make public claims from uncertain ecology.
- Do not duplicate public video embeds or structured records in Markdown.
